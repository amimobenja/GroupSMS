GroupSMS

It's an android application that enables you to send personalized text messages to a group of people.

You can register individuals manually using the registration portal on the screen or individuals wishing to register can do so by sending you short code such as;

REG: Their_First_Name Their_Second_Name Their_Nick_Name Group_Number

The Group_Number is an integer of the groups as they appear on the Group drop down menu.
